What is Illustration Design?

Illustration Design is the practice of creating visual images to explain, decorate, or enhance written content or ideas.

Job Roles

In the world of Illustration Design, there are numerous roles one can pursue after receiving a bachelor's degree, associate’s degree, or some college training. These include: Graphic Designer, Concept Artist, Book Illustrator, Medical Illustrator, and Advertising Designer
