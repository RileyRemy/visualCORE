What is Animation Design?

Animation design is the process of creating moving images and visual effects for various media, including film, television, video games, and digital products, using both 2D and 3D techniques.

Job Roles

In the world of animation, there are numerous roles one can pursue after receiving their bachelor's degree or associate's degree. Some of these include: Animator, Storyboard Artist, Character Designer, Art Director, and 3D Modeler.
