Graphic Design

What is Graphic Design?

Graphic design the art of using visual elements like typography, images, colors, and shapes to communicate information and convey a message effectively.

Job Roles

In the world of graphic design, there are numerous roles one can pursue after receiving a bachelor's degree, associate’s degree, or some college training. These include: Graphic Designer, Web Designer, UX/UI Designer, Art Director, Creative Director, and Packaging Designer
