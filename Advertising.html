What is Advertising Design?

Advertising design is the art of creating visually compelling and persuasive materials to promote products, services, or brands—aiming to grab attention, convey messages effectively, and ultimately drive consumer action.

Job Roles

In the world of advertising, there are numerous roles one can pursue after receiving a bachelor's degree, associate’s degree, or some college training. These include: Graphic Designer, Art Director, Copywriter, UX Designer, Motion Graphics Artist, Web Designer, and Advertising Manager.
